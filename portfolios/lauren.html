Lauren Platzker is a Jewish American designer and illustrator born and raised in the Bay Area, California. The combination of both their practices create unique and versatile work expanding into many different platforms and mediums. Lauren works with their child-like imagination and inspirations from loved graphic movements such as New Wave and Memphis to create bold, shape-based, and narrative pieces that evoke nostalgia as well as joy. When not making, Lauren can be seen feeding raisins to their pet bunny/best friend Colombo.
