Lauren Platzker is a Jewish American designer and illustrator born and raised in California's Bay Area. The synergy between their design and illustration practices creates a unique and versatile body of work expanding into many different platforms and mediums. Lauren works with their playful imagination and inspirations from movements like New Wave and Memphis to create bold, shape-based visuals, and narrative designs that evoke nostalgia and joy. When not making, Lauren can be seen feeding raisins to their pet bunny/best friend Colombo. Say hi at lplatzker@gmail.com.
